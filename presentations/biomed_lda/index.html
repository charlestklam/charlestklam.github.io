Understanding Biomedical Research with Latent Dirichlet Allocation

This project applies LDA to a corpus of qualitative biomedical studies sourced from PubMed. The goal is to discover what broad research themes emerge when qualitative methods are used.

LDA is a machine learning technique that automatically groups documents by their underlying themes
